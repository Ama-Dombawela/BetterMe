Volunteer for Better Me

Join our community of volunteers dedicated to promoting health and well-being. Your time and skills can make a significant difference in people's lives!

Community Wellness Workshops

Lead wellness workshops in underserved communities, teaching basic health practices, nutrition, and mental health awareness.

  • Commitment: 4-8 hours/week
  • Location: Various community centers
  • Impact: 200+ individuals monthly

Elderly Fitness Companion

Support seniors in maintaining active lifestyles by accompanying them on walks, helping with gentle exercises, and providing social interaction.

  • Commitment: 2-6 hours/week
  • Location: Senior centers & homes
  • Impact: 50+ seniors weekly

Mental Health Helpline

Provide support and resources to those experiencing mental health challenges through our dedicated helpline service.

  • Commitment: 4 hours/week
  • ocation: Remote (from home)
  • Impact: 100+ callers weekly

Nutrition Education Program

Educate children and families about healthy eating habits through interactive workshops and cooking demonstrations.

  • Commitment: 3-5 hours/week
  • Location: Schools & community centers
  • Impact: 150+ individuals monthly
